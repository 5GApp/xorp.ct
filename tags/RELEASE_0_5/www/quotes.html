A search on Google turned up this quote:

Peperdos' moustache drooped. "I fear, my son, even should you come to possess all this virtue, you cannot help the world. For the highest virtue of all has vanished entirely, and people nowadays scarcely know what it is anymore."

The saucer-boy was afraid to ask what this cardinal virtue was, for fear of being lumped in with "people nowadays."

"Xorp," Peperdos answered, without waiting for a question. "Xorp has vanished from the world. Our statesmen and generals in the Army hardly know what Xorp is anymore. The young generation have forsaken Xorp entirely. We are in a Xorpless age, my son, and without Xorp, mighty deeds will not be done."

From Konstantinople by Matt Amati.
